## Can LLM-Generated Text Empower Surgical Vision-Language Pre-training?

**Chengan Che***, **Chao Wang***, Jiayuan Huang, Xinyue Chen, Luis C. Garcia-Peraza-Herrera

**CVPR Workshops 2026, Oral Presentation**

SurgLIME investigates whether LLM-generated descriptions can support scalable surgical vision-language pre-training. It uses parameter-efficient visual-language alignment and a confidence mechanism that reduces the influence of unreliable generated text.

\* Equal contribution.
