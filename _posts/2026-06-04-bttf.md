## Back to the Feature: Explaining Video Classifiers with Video Counterfactual Explanations

Chao Wang, **Chengan Che**, Xinyue Chen, Sophia Tsoka, Luis C. Garcia-Peraza-Herrera

**CVPR 2026**

Back to the Feature explains video classifiers by generating counterfactual examples in feature space. The method identifies changes that alter model predictions while preserving the temporal and semantic structure of the original video.
