## Can LLM-Generated Text Empower Surgical Vision-Language Pre-training?

**Chengan Che***, **Chao Wang***, Jiayuan Huang, Xinyue Chen, Luis C. Garcia-Peraza-Herrera

**CVPR Workshops 2026, Oral Presentation**

SurgLIME enables scalable surgical vision-language pre-training using LLM-generated textual descriptions. It combines parameter-efficient vision-language alignment with a confidence-aware mechanism that reduces the contribution of less reliable generated text during training.

\* Equal contribution.
